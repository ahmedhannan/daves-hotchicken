Choose Your Heat

The 7 Dave's Spice Levels Explained

Every slider and tender comes in one of seven heat levels. The heat builds as you eat — so first-timers should order one level below what they think they can handle. Medium is the most popular pick and delivers the authentic Nashville hot chicken experience.

Level 1

No Spice

Pure crispy Nashville chicken flavor — juicy, golden, zero heat. The go-to for kids.

Level 2

Lite Mild

A gentle whisper of warmth. Very manageable — the right entry point for spice-sensitive eaters.

Level 3

Mild

Flavor-forward with a friendly kick. The recommended starting point for most first-timers.

Level 4

Medium 🔥

The crowd favorite. Real Nashville balance of bold flavor and honest burn.

Level 5

Hot

Where the chili heat kicks in hard. Order extra Dave's Sauce or honey to balance.

Level 6

Extra Hot

Intentional, lingering, full-face heat. Only order this on purpose.

Level 7

Reaper ☠️

Carolina Reaper extract. Most locations require a signed waiver. Always have a shake ready.

🌶️Pro pairing hack: always grab extra Dave's Sauce or honey on the side. If you've gone too hot, a shake kills the burn far faster than water or soda ever will.

The Story

What Is Dave's Hot Chicken?

Dave's Hot Chicken started in 2017 as a scrappy pop-up in an East Hollywood parking lot, built by four childhood friends — Arman Oganesyan, Tom Rubin, Gary Rubin, and Dave Henig — with a combined $900 budget and a passion for Nashville-style hot chicken. Word spread fast, the lines got long, and the concept exploded.

Today it's one of the fastest-growing hot chicken chains in the country, with 900+ locations across the United States and Canada. Despite the rapid growth, the formula has stayed simple: crispy Nashville-style tenders and sliders, seven customizable spice levels, and bold flavor that punches well above its price.

Whether you're a spice-averse beginner ordering No Spice for the crunch alone, or a fearless Reaper-chaser who signs the waiver with a smirk, the menu is built so everyone at the table can order exactly the heat they want.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Dave's Hot Chicken menu prices in 2026?

Prices range from about $3.50 for a single tender up to $64.99 for the largest Hot Box. The most popular combo — 2 Sliders with Fries — runs around $14.99. Prices vary slightly by location, and third-party delivery apps add service fees on top.

What spice level should I choose for my first visit?

Start with Mild or Medium. Medium is the most popular pick and gives you the authentic Nashville experience. Lite Mild is the right call if you're spice-sensitive. Order one level below what you think you can handle — the heat builds bite by bite.

Does Dave's Hot Chicken have vegetarian options?

Yes — the full "Dave's NOT Chicken" cauliflower line covers Cauli Sliders, Cauli Tenders, and Cauli Tender Bites at every spice level, including Reaper. Same crunch, same heat. Most cauli items still contain milk and eggs — vegetarian but not fully vegan.

How many calories are in a typical Dave's meal?

A typical combo runs 900–1,310 calories. The 2 Sliders with Fries combo is 1,270 calories; 2 Tenders with Fries is 980. A single tender is 550 and a single slider is 680. Kale slaw at 270 calories is the lightest side. Spice level doesn't significantly change calorie counts.

Is heat free at every level?

Yes — all seven spice levels are included at no extra cost. Heat never changes the price. You can also set a different spice level on each individual item within the same combo, at no additional charge.

Can I order Dave's Hot Chicken for delivery?

Yes. Dave's is on DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub, or order through the Dave's app for rewards points. Delivery fees typically run $2.99–$5.99 plus tip. For the lowest price and crispiest breading, pickup from the app beats delivery every time.

What is the Reaper spice level?

The Reaper is the hottest level, made with Carolina Reaper pepper extract. It's significantly more intense than Extra Hot, and most locations require you to sign a waiver before ordering. Always have a vanilla or chocolate shake ready — dairy neutralizes capsaicin far more effectively than water or soda.

Can I mix spice levels in one combo?

Yes — this is one of Dave's most popular features. In any multi-piece combo, you can set a completely different spice level on each individual tender or slider. One at No Spice for a kid, one at Hot for you — all in the same box.

Does Dave's Hot Chicken have a kids menu?

Dave's doesn't have a dedicated kids menu, but No Spice tenders and sliders are perfect for children — all the crispy Nashville-style flavor with zero heat. Pair with crinkle fries and a vanilla shake for a complete kids meal.

What makes Dave's different from other hot chicken chains?

Three things stand out: the seven-level spice scale (most competitors max at three or four), the ability to mix heat levels in the same combo, and consistent quality at scale. Dave's also keeps the menu focused — fewer items, executed consistently, at a price that punches above its weight.

What are the accepted payment methods at Dave's?

Most locations accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. The Dave's app stores payment methods for quick in-app ordering. Gift cards are available through the app and at participating locations.

Is Dave's the same as Nashville hot chicken?

Dave's serves Nashville-style hot chicken — the tradition built around a cayenne-heavy dry spice rub applied to crispy fried chicken. Dave's adds its own proprietary blend and seven-tier heat scale, but the core technique and flavor heritage are rooted in the Nashville tradition.
